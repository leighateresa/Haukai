The Haukai Menu

Our delicious kai

Before colonisation, Māori lived off the land and the sea. Even today, Kai Moana (seafood) is a staple in traditional Māori cuisine. Come in to experience our take on this tradional Māori kai including our delicious Fish of the Day and Seafood Chowder amongst other Māori dishes.
